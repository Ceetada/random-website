/ how it works

Three stages, from pixels to a pin

Every query flows through the same deterministic pipeline. Nothing leaves your machine.

  1. 01

    Retrieval

    The query image becomes an 8448-dim descriptor via MegaLoc (DINOv2 ViT-B/14), PCA-reduced to 1024, then compared against the index to shortlist the top 500 candidate locations.

  2. 02

    Matching

    MASt3R performs dense, 3D-aware pixel correspondence between your photo and each candidate — surfacing hundreds of reliable matches even from partial overlap.

  3. 03

    Consensus

    Matches are clustered into ~50 m grid cells and scored on spatial coherence. The most self-consistent cluster wins and yields the final coordinate.

Specs & honest limits

  • LanguagePython 3.10+
  • RetrievalMegaLoc · DINOv2 ViT-B/14
  • MatchingMASt3R (ECCV 2024)

Know before you rely on it

  • Accuracy is coverage-dependent — no index, no fix.
  • Repetitive architecture can produce false positives.
  • Processing takes minutes per query, not real-time.
